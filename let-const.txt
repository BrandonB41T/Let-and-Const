What is the difference between var and let?
With var, you can reassign and redeclare variables, but you cannot redeclare with let. Also, let has a block scope and no hoisting characteristic.

What is the difference between var and const?
Var allows you to reassign and redeclare variables, const does not allow either. Also, like let, const has a block scope and no hoisting characteristic.

What is the difference between let and const?
Let allows reassigning, const does not.

What is hoisting?
Hoisting is when variable declarations are ran prior to the rest of the code; it happens when you use var to declare a variable.
